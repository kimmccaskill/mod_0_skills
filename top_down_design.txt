How to Become a Turing Student

1 Get into school
  1.1 Apply for program
    1.1.1 Pick FEE or BEE
      1.1.1.1 Google information about different paths
      1.1.1.2 Go back and forth a million times
    1.1.2 Fill out application
      1.1.2.1 Fill out personal information
        1.1.2.1.1 Upload resume or fill out questionnaire
      1.1.2.2 Take logic test portion
    1.1.3 Get invited for an interview
      1.1.3.1 Set up time with interviewer
      1.1.3.2 Attend interview
        1.1.3.2.1 Talk about self
        1.1.3.2.2 Take logic test
    1.1.4 Get accepted
2 Pay tuition
  2.1 Decide on how you are going to pay for school and life
    2.1.2 Cry because you will be poor
  2.2 Apply for loan
    2.2.1 Decide on one of the many loan options
    2.2.2 Decide on how much money
    2.2.3 Apply
      2.2.3.1 Go to website and fill out application
3 Make commitment
  3.1 Quit job
    3.1.1 Put in 1 month notice
      3.1.1.1 Have long discussion with boss
    3.1.2 Notify coworkers
      3.1.2.1 Plan going away party
    3.1.3 Wait and work anxiously until last day of work
      3.1.3.1 Count down days on calendar
      3.1.3.2 Get all tasks done
      3.1.3.3 Create guide for next victim taking your position
  3.2 Plan finances
    3.2.1 Manage income
      3.2.1.1 Count every cent you have
      3.2.1.2 Count every cent you will get from loans
    3.2.2 Calculate spendings
      3.2.2.1 Calculate total cost of rent over 7 months
      3.2.2.2 Calculate average cost of living per day
  3.3 Break up with girlfriend
    3.3.1 Procrastinate
      3.3.1.1 Make plans to work it out
        3.3.1.1.1 Talk about different ways to make it work
        3.3.1.1.2 Talk about how those plans won't work
      3.3.1.2 Avoid talking about your relationship for weeks
        3.3.1.2.1 Pretend school isn't happening soon when discussing future
    3.3.2 Break up with girlfriend
      3.3.2.1 Prepare for inevitable crying
      3.3.2.2 Argue
      3.3.2.3 Make up
      3.3.2.4 Tell her that we can "take a break"
