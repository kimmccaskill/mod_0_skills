Class: Employee

Attributes:
hireDate (date)
position (string)
hourlyWage (float)
inTraining (boolean)

Methods:
getPromotion (change position and increase hourlyWage + 5)
finishTraining (changes inTraining to false after hireDate plus 90 days)
getRaise (increases hourlyWage + 1)
employementLength (takes current date - hireDate)
